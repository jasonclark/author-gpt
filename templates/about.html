About - What is an Author?

What is an Author? is a sample application to demonstrate how Large Language Models can generate text. It uses GPT2 to allow someone to prompt the model to write and compose.

Use the form to ask questions and engage with the AIsistant. You can create your own line of writing. Some sample opening lines:

  • It was a queer, sultry summer, the summer they electrocuted the Rosenbergs, and I didn't know what I was doing in New York.
  • It is a truth universally acknowledged, that a single man in possession of a good fortune must be in want of a wife.
  • This is my favorite book in all the world, though I have never read it.
  • It was a bright cold day in April, and the clocks were striking thirteen.
  • We were somewhere around Barstow on the edge of the desert when the drugs began to take hold.
